When I decided to make my own portfolio 3 years ago, I was deep into [Eleventy](https://www.11ty.dev/) for other pet projects so I figured that it would be smart to stay on that technology.  
To this day I still think that was a good decision.

But I noticed back then that there was no theme nor starter kit that offered what I needed. So that's when I decided to create my own for myself and others who might need one like this.

This is how [Niepce](https://github.com/GoOz/Niepce) was born.

And now, 3 years later, it needs an update. I need to update it.

I hope you'll like it, I know I do.
