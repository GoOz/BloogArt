title: To choose is to give up
date: 2026-04-14
---

I was invited to apply for a future exhibition in October. As rewarding as it seems, I can't feel anything but doubt and pressure. My fault, really.

Now I got to choose five pictures. A series of 5 pictures. And to this day I got 885 pictures in my portfolio.
Sure not all are exhibition material but still.

Difficult choices, just that.

I narrowed down to [three series](https://www.foojin.com/series.html) so far. Still not sure though.

I shoot what I like and I like a _lot_ of stuff, but I don't know what people like, so I'm not sure which series I should choose for the application, which one would give the most chances of being selected.

Any thoughts?
